Linux manual pages Section 7  

Name

operator — C operator precedence and order of evaluation

DESCRIPTION

This manual page lists C operators and their precedence in evaluation.

Operator Associativity
() [] −> . left to right
! ~ ++ −− + − (type) * & sizeof right to left
* / % left to right
+ − left to right
<< >> left to right
< <= > >= left to right
== != left to right
& left to right
^ left to right
| left to right
&& left to right
|| left to right
?: right to left
= += −= *= /= %= <<= >>= &= ^= |= right to left
, left to right
